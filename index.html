About Presentonomica

The Premier Economics Conclave

The School of Economics, XIM University, is proud to host its National Flagship Event - Presentonomica. This one-day event brings together high school, undergraduate, and postgraduate students from across the country to present innovative solutions to real-world economic challenges. It's a platform for collaboration, competition, and intellectual growth.

What to Expect:

  • Competitions & Simulations challenging analytical reasoning.
  • Expert Talks & Panel Discussions with industry leaders.
  • Interactive Activities & Exhibitions.
  • A platform to network with the brightest minds in economics.

Event Details:

Date: 9th October, 2025

Reporting Time: 9:00 AM - 9:15 AM

Event Starts: 10:00 AM

Venue: Mini Auditorium, New Academic Block, XIM University, Plot No:12(A), Nijigada, Kurki, Harirajpur, Jatani, Odisha 752050

Presentation Topics

  • Universal Basic Income: Analyzing the potential impact of UBI on overall economic growth.
  • Liberalization, Privatization and Globalization: Then vs Now.
  • Tariffs as Instruments of Economic Defense: Economic Strategy or Disguised Geopolitical Warfare?
  • Aging Population vs Upcoming Youth: Prospects for the future of India.
  • Analysis of the workings of Foreign Aid: Effectiveness and Potential Economic Distortions.
  • Tech Job Outsourcing in the Context of H1-B Visa: Economic Implications in the short and long run.
  • The impact of AI on Gen Z Workforce.
  • Construct a systems dynamics model of the water-energy-food nexus in Northern India.
  • Analyze and criticize government sponsored social security plans with respect to free market economic theory.
  • Assume an economic policy framework balancing monetary policy autonomy, exchange rate stability, and unrestricted digital monetary assets.
  • Examine the role of government scholarships for higher education in modifying household spending choice.

Rules and Regulations

Registration

All participants must register before the specified deadline. Late entries will not be entertained.

Team Composition

Presentation participants must form a team of three. Prize money is awarded to teams, not individuals.

Presentation Time

Each team will have 8 minutes for their presentation, followed by a 2-minute Q&A session with the judges.

Originality

Any form of plagiarism will lead to immediate disqualification. All work must be original.

Dress Code

Participants are required to be in business formals throughout the event.

Identification

Participants must carry their institution's ID card on the event day.

Prizes & Recognition

Attractive cash prizes to be won across all presentation categories. Prizes are awarded to the winning team.
